Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first survey?
Click "Create Survey" in your dashboard, choose a template or start from scratch, add your questions, and publish. You can also use our Quick Survey feature for faster creation.
When do I get paid for completing tasks?
CPA offers are credited within 24-48 hours after completion. YouTube tasks are verified and credited within 24 hours. Minimum payout is $10 via PayPal.
How many surveys can I create?
Free users can create up to 3 surveys. Pro users get unlimited surveys. Enterprise users get unlimited everything plus advanced features.
Can I share surveys with my team?
Yes! Pro and Enterprise users can invite team members, assign roles, and collaborate on surveys. Go to Team Management in your dashboard.
What languages are supported?
We support 25+ African languages including English, Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, Zulu, Amharic, and more. You can create multilingual surveys to reach diverse communities.
How do I track my earnings?
Check your earnings in the Settings > Earnings section or on your dashboard. You'll see total earned, pending amounts, and detailed transaction history.
